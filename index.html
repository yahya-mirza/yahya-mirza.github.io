Yahya Mirza

Mechanical Engineering Student @ UC San Diego | Robotics & Mechatronics

Hey I'm Yahya, nice to meet you! Currently, I am in the process of an end-to-end production for a linear slider mechanism for Yonder Dynamics, UCSD's competition Mars Rover team, implementing statics analysis, GD&T, and DFM principles. On top of that, over this past quarter my peers and I successfully built a competition soccer robot utilizing risk reduction principles, receiving the "Best Manufacturing" award and nominated for "Best Technical Presentation" out of 34 teams. In addition, I am the co-founder and former project lead for Foothill College's award-winning FireFlight RC Plane, an ML-Driven, aerial sensing RC Plane for wildfire prediction and evacuation. At home, I am working on numerous projects, including a WiFi and sensor-based automatic plant watering apparatus, in which you can water multiple plants in your home without having to touch it! I am proficient in SOLIDWORKS, Arduino, Fusion 360, OnShape, and MATLAB, while also being well versed in microcontrollers and other electronics. Feel free to reach out if you are interested in anything mechanical design, machining, or technology!
